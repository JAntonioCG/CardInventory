Bringing the world together through Pokémon

Our Mission

The world of Pokémon connects people across the globe, beloved by kids, adults, and every Trainer in between! These incredible creatures have crossed borders and language barriers to reach the hearts of millions for over 25 years, bringing people together through the joy of play and discovery.

Welcome to The Pokémon Company International The Pokémon Company International (TPCi) has a mission to delight our fans through excellent products and meaningful experiences. We hope to help make the world a better place for everyone by creating opportunities to bond and grow through shared journeys and friendly competition.

What do we offer?

  • Complete Catalog: Explore an extensive catalog of cards from all expansions, from classic editions to the most recent.
  • Custom Tools: Create wish lists, value your cards and follow market prices to make informed decisions.
  • Active Community: Join our community and share your passion for Pokémon with other collectors.
  • Educational Resources: Learn everything you need to know about the world of Pokémon cards, from their history to playing strategies.

Why choose us?

  • Protect your investment: Make smart decisions thanks to our card valuation tool and historical price data.
  • Discover hidden treasures: Find the cards you are looking for with our powerful search tools.
  • Be part of something big: Join our community and participate in exclusive events and contests.
